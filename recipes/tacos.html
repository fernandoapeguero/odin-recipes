Tacos de chorizo

Tacos de chorizo special in house sauce that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Chorizo
  • 2 X Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Cook teh chorizo with tomato sauce for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat the pan and leave soft shell for 1 minute each side.
  3. Cut the lettuce, tomato, and lemon.
  4. Blend the chorizo sauce.
  5. Bring it all together and to your liking.
